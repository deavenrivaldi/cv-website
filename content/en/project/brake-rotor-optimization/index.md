## **Overview**
This project investigates different brake rotor designs to improve heat dissipation and reduce brake fade in high-speed conditions.

> Improves braking reliability by optimizing heat dissipation in high-speed conditions.
---

## **Problem**
- Brake fade caused by excessive heat buildup
- Reduced braking performance leading to safety risks

---

## **Solution**
I analyzed and compared multiple rotor designs to identify the most effective heat dissipation configuration.

---

## **System Design**
### Design Variants
- Solid disc
- Drilled vented disc
- Slotted vented disc

### Analysis Focus
- Heat dissipation efficiency
- Friction and braking performance

---

## Engineering Concepts
- Heat transfer
- Friction and torque analysis

### Tools & Technologies
- Autodesk Inventor
- Fusion 360

---

## **Key Features**
- Comparative design evaluation
- Performance-driven mechanical analysis

---

## **Outcome**
- Identified drilled vented disc as the most efficient design
- Provided design insights for improved braking systems
