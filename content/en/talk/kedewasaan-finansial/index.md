abstract: "This talk provides a structured introduction to financial literacy and investment fundamentals, aimed at helping individuals develop sustainable financial habits and make informed decisions. It begins by highlighting the importance of financial awareness in personal and professional life, followed by practical approaches to budgeting, saving, and expense management.The session then introduces key investment concepts, including risk profiling, investment horizons, and common financial instruments. Different investment strategies are discussed based on individual risk tolerance, along with fundamental risk management principles such as diversification and asset allocation. Finally, the talk explores broader perspectives on asset and wealth management, emphasizing long-term planning and disciplined decision-making as essential components of financial maturity."
