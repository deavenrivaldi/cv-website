## **Overview**
This project integrates multiple safety and comfort features into a single embedded system prototype to address common driving risks and environmental conditions.

> Improves driving safety and comfort through integrated sensing, control, and automation systems.
---

## **Problem**
- High rate of traffic accidents
- Poor visibility during night driving
- Unsafe cabin temperatures when parked
- Lack of integrated low-cost safety systems

---

## **Solution**
I designed a **multi-functional smart car prototype** incorporating safety, automation, and monitoring features.

---

## **System Design**
### Safety Systems
- Cruise control with distance-based regulation
- Collision prevention with warning and automatic braking

### Comfort System
Cabin cooling activated based on temperature threshold

### Automation
Automatic headlight control based on ambient light

### Monitoring
Dashboard displaying:
- Cabin temperature
- Light intensity
- Distance from surrounding objects

---

## **Engineering Concepts**
- Signal processing (FFT, filtering, Hamming window)
- Control systems (PID, closed-loop control)

### Tools & Technologies
- Microcontroller systems
- Sensors (distance, temperature, light)
- ThingsBoard (dashboard visualization)

---

## **Key Features**
- 360° distance monitoring
- Closed-loop control with PID for stability
- Integrated multi-system architecture

---

## **Outcome**
- Successfully trained and tested mask detection model
- Demonstrated feasibility for public safety applications
